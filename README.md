# MindNote
MindNote is an elegant, simple, smooth, and efficient mind mapping app designed to help you think clearly, plan effectively, and organize your ideas visually. We combined mindmap and markdown syntax, which allow users to creat mindmap automatically by importing markdown content.
# MindNote Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 12 2026.
## Data Collection
MindNote does not collect any personal data. All data will be stored at your device(s) and iCloud(if enabled). We have no access to your data.
We do not track users, serve ads, or share data with third parties.
## iCloud Sync
If you enable iCloud, your mind maps are stored in your personal iCloud account (CloudKit private database). 
This data is only accessible to you and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
