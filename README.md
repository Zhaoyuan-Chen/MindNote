# MindNote
MindNote is an elegant, simple, smooth, and efficient mind mapping app designed to help you think clearly, plan effectively, and organize your ideas visually.
# MindNote Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026
## Data Collection
MindNote does not collect any personal data. 
We do not track users, serve ads, or share data with third parties.
## iCloud Sync
If you enable iCloud, your mind maps are stored in your personal iCloud account (CloudKit private database). 
This data is only accessible to you and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
## Local Storage
All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We have no access to your data.
